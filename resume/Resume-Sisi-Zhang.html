About关于我

I find the signals others miss. Over seven years in sportswear, retail, and QSR — including five years shaping Nike's Kids and Women's strategies — I've built a career on reading markets and translating what I see into products people want. I pioneered AI-enabled research workflows before they became common practice, identified whitespace opportunities that launched entirely new categories, and turned complex data into narratives that move cross-functional teams to action. I'm driven by an enduring curiosity: a belief that the best insights come from asking better questions, learning new tools, and never settling for the obvious answer. 我发现别人忽略的信号。在运动、零售和快餐行业的七年里——包括五年为 Nike 童装和女装品类制定策略——我一直在读懂市场、将洞察转化为消费者真正需要的产品。我在 AI 研究工作流尚未成为主流时就率先实践，发现空白机会推动全新品类诞生，将复杂数据转化为推动跨职能团队行动的叙事。驱动我的是一种持久的好奇心：相信最好的洞察来自于提出更好的问题、学习新工具，以及永不满足于显而易见的答案。

Women's Fleece "Drop Shoulder Adoption" 女装摇粒绒「落肩设计」

Diagnosed a product structure gap where women's fleece was largely a downsized men's H-shape construction, and demonstrated that drop-shoulder design better aligned with market preference. 诊断产品结构问题——女装摇粒绒基本是男装 H 型版型的缩小版，并论证落肩设计更符合市场偏好。

  • Demonstrated through market and competitor product analysis that authentic oversize preference was better aligned with drop-shoulder design cues. 通过市场和竞品分析，证明真正的 oversize 偏好更适配落肩设计语言。
  • Informed product structure redesign direction and improved category positioning relevance. 指导产品结构重新设计方向，提升品类定位的市场相关性。

The State of Tennis 网球运动现状研究

Decoded the current tennis landscape through three strategic lenses — tournaments, athletes, and products — benchmarking competitors such as Lululemon and Wilson to identify product strategy gaps and inform future direction. 通过赛事、运动员和产品三大战略框架解码当前网球运动格局，对标 Lululemon、Wilson 等竞品，分析产品策略差异，确定未来产品方向。

  • Built a reusable competitive analysis framework structured around tournaments, athletes, and product ecosystems to systematically assess market positioning across the tennis category. 构建了以赛事、运动员和产品生态为核心的可复用竞品分析框架，系统评估网球品类的市场定位。
  • Identified strategic gaps between Nike and key competitors (Lululemon, Wilson) in tennis product offerings, driving targeted improvement recommendations. 发现 Nike 与关键竞品（Lululemon、Wilson）在网球产品线上的战略差距，推动了针对性的改进建议。

Colorway Study 配色策略研究

Analyzed color strategies across runway collections and social media OOTD content to surface trending colorways in casual menswear, womenswear, and sportswear — the company's first AI agent–powered interactive HTML slide deliverable. 通过分析秀场和社交媒体 OOTD 服装的颜色策略，展现休闲男女装及运动服饰的流行配色趋势——全公司首个与 AI Agent 合作以交互式 HTML 呈现的分析报告。

  • Pioneered an AI agent–powered workflow to produce interactive HTML slide decks, establishing a new presentation format for data-rich color insights across the organization. 率先采用 AI Agent 驱动的工作流生成交互式 HTML 幻灯片，为全公司数据密集型配色洞察建立了全新的呈现方式。
  • Established a systematic color research framework covering runway fashion and social media OOTD trends across casual and sportswear categories, with ongoing deep-dive analysis in progress. 建立了涵盖秀场时装和社交媒体 OOTD 趋势的系统性颜色研究框架，覆盖休闲和运动服饰品类，持续深入分析中。
